# aohsw 

Have you ever found parodies funny? I have.
As a programmer, I enjoy creating parodies of software. aohsw is a collection of such parodies.

## What's inside?

Here you will find various software parodies created for the sake of humor, experimentation, and entertainment.

> ⚠️ All projects are parodies and are not intended to replace the original software (the text "⚠️ All projects are parodies and are not intended to replace the original software" is included just for the sake of formality).
